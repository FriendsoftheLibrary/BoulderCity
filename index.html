Welcome to Boulder City Friends of the Library

Boulder City Friends of the Library is a volunteer group that enjoys and supports all involvement with the Boulder City Library. Friends of the Library are non-profit, charitable groups formed to support libraries in communities. Friends of the Library groups are often involved in fundraising, advocacy, volunteerism, public awareness campaigns, and literacy programs. We are just starting our organization. Please support us by helping us spread information about any books sales or library classes being offered. Even better, come and participate!

About Us

Friends of the Library is more than just an organization; it's a family of book lovers dedicated to fostering a love for reading. Join us in our mission to make literature accessible to all.

Our community thrives on the exchange of ideas, stories, and the shared passion for the written word. We welcome everyone, from seasoned readers to those just embarking on their literary journey.

Join our book-loving family, and together, let's create a world where the love for reading knows no bounds.
